office fuli

浮力工作室成立于上海与鹿特丹，主创成员们分别毕业于哈佛大学与代尔夫特理工大学。自成立起，团队带着国际化的视角与对本土文化的关照，完成了一系列兼具空间品质与城市效益的在地公共项目。同时与优秀的国内外建筑团队合作，积极参与公共类型项目投标，以期通过实践打磨我们对于当代城市空间中诸多复杂挑战的思考和应对。我们期望通过对设计本身的锤炼，保留公共空间作为人与人、自然与城市、平凡生活与艺术遐想之间的纽带之可能。

 

office fuli was founded in Shanghai and Rotterdam, with its core members graduating from Harvard GSD and TU Delft. From the outset, the team has carried out a series of contextual public projects both domestically and internationally, combining global perspectives with local cultural sensitivity to achieve both spatial quality and urban impact. Meanwhile, we have collaborated with leading architectural practices worldwide to actively participate in public project competitions, refining our thinking and responses to the complex challenges of contemporary urban conditions through hands-on practice. We strive to hone our design craft, preserving the potential of public spaces as vital mediators—between people and people, nature and the city, and the everyday life and artistic imagination.
